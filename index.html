How it works

Start light. Build over time.

Health Passport is the teen-friendly front door. It becomes Healthograph Lite in young adulthood and a more complete Healthograph when life and care become more complex.

01

Create your Passport

Answer a few basics: name, age range, emergency contact, allergies, medications, and your health goals.

02

Add to Wallet

Download a mobile Health Passport for check-in and readiness. A premium physical card can follow.

03

Invite a parent

Parents can complete thoughtful details like doctor, immunizations, insurance, and family health notes.

04

Document care

AiTHOS Certified providers can scan your Passport and upload visit notes or care updates within 24 hours.
